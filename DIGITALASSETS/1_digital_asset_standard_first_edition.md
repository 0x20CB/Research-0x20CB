# \[Digital Assets] Digital Asset Standard #1

## Introduction

Digital Assets provide a way of storing information/data/assets in relation to a digital identity. NFTs, or Non-Fungible Tokens, have been considered a digital asset. This paper was made in hopes to define a digital asset, standardize the digital asset with interoperability, and to get more attention towards the value of Digital Assets owned by holders, mainly, in decentralized systems.

In this paper, the **Digital Asset Standard #1 (DAS#1)** will be defined using properties of cryptography among other sources and help to familizarize people with the idea of owning digital assets.
